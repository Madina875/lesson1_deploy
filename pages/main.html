User002

User002 was born under a sky split in two — one half filled with stars and constellations, the other a mesh of glowing circuits. Her world was not a normal one: mountains had mechanical hearts, forests whispered with robotic fireflies, and oceans held serpentine monsters older than the moon. Her parents — a scholar of ancient runes and an engineer of war machines — named her User002, believing she would be a bridge between magic and technology. While other children played with toys, User002 crafted her own: a small fox made of brass and enchanted gears. It could leap over rivers and store memories like a diary. She called it Kyra, her first friend in a land where friends were scarce. At twelve, every child had to face the First Hunt — a rite of passage against the roaming beasts of the steel plains. User002’s opponent was a creature of nightmare: part wolf, part machine, with claws like molten glass. She didn’t kill it. Instead, she hacked its control chip and freed it from the Hunters’ program. It bowed to her before running into the wild. From that day, people whispered: "She tames monsters instead of destroying them."
